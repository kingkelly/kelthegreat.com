The science part
My compromised kidneys means my body is no longer able to filter the waste in my blood. And because my blood type is B+, the only compatible donors are blood type B or O. This means that the wait-list for a ‘deceased’ kidney could take up to 7+ years.

Clearly, this isn’t an option because we don’t have 7+ years. And so, it means I’m relying on the kindness of a live donor if I’m to get a kidney in time.
What do I need?
Most people have two working kidneys but only need one. Which is where you come in. You see, because my blood type is on the rare side, I need a transplant from a live kidney donor. Now that truly would be life-changing. It’s everything I dream of.
